Comparison Between Meat Type

Almost anyone in this world of course consumes meat, but do you know what is the most popular type of meat? Here there will be 4 types of meat to be compared with each other that is poultry, beef, pig and lastly sheep

In this visualisations we will dive deeper into the meat consumption worldwide throughout the years.

First of all, from this lolipop chart we can see that in the year 2022 poultry is leading among all other types of meat with a total value of 133,696 thousand tonnes consumed. Sheep is the lowest with only 15,965 thousand tonnes consumed.

The year slider is available to check at which year how each type of meat was consumed, but its hard to compare at a certain point of the year so let's take a better look in our donut chart.
With the intervals of 10 years since 1990 we have seen a significance changes in the meat type consumption throughout the years.

In the 1990s we can see that pig was the most consumed type of meat with a total of 41.25%. poultry was the 3rd most consumed with 22.47%.

We can see the changes clearly that more people are consuming poultry it even surpasses beef in the year 2000 being the 2nd most consumed with 29.82%. pig still stays on the top with 39.28%

2010 datas ranking seems to be similar with 2000, the more interesting information is in 2020. Surprisingly poultry becomes the most consumed type of meat among all of the others with total percentage of 40.51%.

Growing Trend In Meat Consumption

The growing trend between these 4 types of meat is depicted from this line chart. Highlight of the visualisation is how massive people have grown to eat poultry.

Poultry consumption surpasses beef in 1996, and also later in 2016 surpasses pig being the highest of all.

It is not quite a surprise that sheep meat consumption is lowest of all

OECD Countries Meat Consumption

Previous visualisations are all for worldwide, but how about OECD Countries?

This map visualises the consumptions of the OECD countries throughout the world, the color represents the meat type and the sizes tells us which country consumes more as the symbol grows bigger.

Starting from America, the northern part country that consumes the most in all type of meat is the United States. While in the southern part Brazil is the highest consumer.

Whereas in Europe, Russia is the top consumer for all type of meat.
In Asia, the most consuming meat country is China. If compared to the other countries China consumes way more than the other countries. In fact, China is the country that consumes the most meat in the world

Lastly Africa's most consuming country is countries in South africa, but Nigeria consumes more sheep meat than South africa countries which is an interesting point to note.

Year slider interactivity is given so that users can see the sizes in certain years, drop-down box is also provided to pick the type of meats.
